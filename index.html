“Infrastructure as Code”

This approach involves managing infrastructure, including its setup and configuration, using code, which ensures predictability, reproducibility and versioning.

"Continuous Deployment"

"Continuous deployment"

an automated process that enables the rapid and reliable deployment of new versions of applications and infrastructure changes. "DevOps is a mindset, not a tool"

this phrase reminds us that DevOps is not a set of specific technologies, but a certain approach to work that involves a culture of collaboration, responsibility, and continuous improvement.

"Collaboration, not just automationl"

is a reminder of the importance of teamwork and knowledge sharing in DevOps, where automation is just one tool to achieve goals.
